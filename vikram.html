Entertainment Business

1. Trees (BST / AVL / Red-Black)

They keep customer or booking data sorted and allow quick searching for show times, slots, or items.

2. Heaps

Useful for managing priority queues like fast-filling shows, game waitlists, or event rankings.

3. BFS / DFS

Gaming maps, room connections, or arena layouts can be modeled as graphs and explored efficiently.

4. Sorting Algorithms

Used for ordering seat numbers, food orders, game scores, or item lists by time/price/popularity.

5. Trie

Enables fast prefix-based search for movies, games, or menu items with autocomplete.
