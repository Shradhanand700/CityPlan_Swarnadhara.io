*Blue & Green Economy Ideas*

1. *Green Energy & Ocean Energy Hub*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

A hub that develops, demonstrates and deploys ocean-based renewable energy (offshore wind, tidal, wave, OTEC) together with energy storage, grid integration and supply‑chain services. The hub can include R&D labs, manufacturing/assembly at portside, testing sites and workforce training.

Impact on the economy
  • Creates high‑skill jobs (engineering, marine construction, operations & maintenance).
  • Improves energy security and reduces fossil‑fuel imports.
  • Attracts investment and develops local supply chains (manufacturing, ports, cables).
  • Enables exportable technologies and services (design, installation, O&M).
Related SDGs
SDG 7 SDG 9 SDG 11 SDG 13 SDG 14

2. *Clean Water + Environmental Industries*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

Companies and projects that deliver water purification (including decentralized systems), desalination, wastewater treatment, reuse, pollution‑control technologies and environmental monitoring services to protect ecosystems and ensure water security.

Impact on the economy
  • Secures water supply for households, agriculture and industry — increasing productivity.
  • Reduces public health burden by preventing water‑borne diseases.
  • Generates manufacturing and service jobs in engineering, operations and monitoring.
  • Enables industrial expansion where reliable water is available.
Related SDGs
SDG 6 SDG 3 SDG 11 SDG 12 SDG 14

3. *Transport model and logistics*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

Integrated transport solutions spanning electric buses & ferries, smart public transport, intermodal freight, green last‑mile delivery, port logistics and digital supply‑chain platforms that reduce emissions and improve efficiency.

Impact on the economy
  • Lowers logistics costs and improves competitiveness for exporters and local businesses.
  • Creates jobs across vehicle manufacturing, fleet operations, warehousing and IT services.
  • Reduces congestion and time lost, increasing productivity in cities.
  • Supports growth of trade by improving port and inland connectivity.
Related SDGs
SDG 9 SDG 11 SDG 13 SDG 8

4. *Ship building and ship repair business*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

Facilities that design, construct, retrofit and repair vessels — from fishing boats to commercial ships and offshore platforms. Includes shipyards, specialized workshops and naval architecture services.

Impact on the economy
  • Provides large numbers of skilled and semi‑skilled jobs (welders, fitters, engineers).
  • Builds industrial capacity for maritime trade and naval requirements.
  • Creates demand for steel, engines, electronics and marine sub‑suppliers.
  • Supports port ecosystem and regional exports through improved fleet availability.

5. *Waste-to-Blue-Energy Solutions*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

Converting coastal and marine waste (seaweed, organic coastal biomass, fishing residues, plastics) into energy carriers (biofuel, biogas, hydrogen, electricity) using biochemical, thermochemical and advanced recycling processes.

Impact on the economy
  • Enables circular value chains — turning pollutants into marketable energy and products.
  • Reduces cleanup costs and restores tourism/fisheries value along coastlines.
  • Stimulates startups in biotech, recycling and green chemistry.
  • Creates local energy sources for coastal communities and industries.
Related SDGs
SDG 12 SDG 7 SDG 13 SDG 14 SDG 11

6. *Deep Ocean Mining — Rare Earth & Critical Minerals*

Brief description • Economic impact • Related SDGs
Description

Carefully governed extraction of polymetallic nodules and mineral deposits from the seabed (cobalt, nickel, manganese, rare earth elements) to supply the supply chain for batteries, electronics and renewable technologies — with strict environmental safeguards and monitoring.

Impact on the economy
  • Secures strategic supplies for high‑tech manufacturing (EVs, energy storage, electronics).
  • Creates high‑tech jobs in robotics, marine robotics and subsea engineering.
  • Generates export revenue while reducing import dependence for critical minerals.
  • Requires investment in monitoring, regulation and environmental impact mitigation — creating additional service sectors.
Related SDGs
SDG 9 SDG 12 SDG 7 SDG 14 SDG 8
