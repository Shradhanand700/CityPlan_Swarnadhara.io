Business Data Structures

Entertainment Business

Trees (BST / AVL / Red-Black)

They keep customer or booking data sorted and allow quick searching for show times, slots, or items.

Heaps

Useful for managing priority queues like fast-filling shows, game waitlists, or event rankings.

BFS / DFS

Gaming maps, room connections, or arena layouts can be modeled as graphs and explored efficiently.

Sorting Algorithms

Used for ordering seat numbers, food orders, game scores, or lists by time/price/popularity.

Trie

Enables fast prefix-based search for movies, games, or menu items with autocomplete.

Restaurant / Food Business

AVL / Red-Black Trees

Keep customer records, bookings, and memberships balanced and searchable with fast updates.

Heap

Manages appointment priority, table reservations, or urgent orders in an efficient way.

Trie

Quickly finds menu items or services when users type partial names.

Skip List

Provides fast updates for dynamic menus, staff availability, or changing time slots.

Sorting Algorithms

Helps rank customers, sort prices, arrange menu items, or order services.

Education Business

Segment Tree

Efficient for range queries like total attendance or marks within a roll-number interval.

AVL / RB Trees

Good for maintaining sorted student data, records, and enrolling/dropping updates quickly.

Trie

Speeds up searching in digital libraries, documents, or course names using prefix lookup.

BFS / DFS

Useful for navigating course prerequisites, academic hierarchies, or campus structures.

Dijkstra / Bellman-Ford / Floyd

Used for shortest-path problems like campus navigation or timetable conflict optimization.

Union-Find

Helps group students, detect cycles in prerequisite graphs, or manage club/team memberships.

Sorting Algorithms

Required for ranking students, sorting marks, listing courses, or arranging schedules.

E-Commerce Business

Trie

Fast prefix search for product names, addresses, categories, and auto-completing user input.

Heap

Prioritizes deliveries (express first), handles limited stock alerts, and processes urgent orders.

Graph Algorithms

Calculate shortest/cheapest shipping routes and optimize delivery paths between warehouses.

Kruskal + Union-Find

Build the most cost-efficient shipping/warehouse network using minimum spanning trees.

Segment / Fenwick Tree

Supports real-time updates on inventory and fast range queries across large product datasets.

Skip List

Maintains sorted dynamic lists of products by price, rating, or delivery speed.

Sorting Algorithms

Help order products by cost, popularity, reviews, or arrival time.
